Lab 4 - Pseudocoding and Problem-solving

Challenge

Assignment was to plan code for a game of choice, of which we picked Rock Paper Scissors. After choosing, we were to write the pseudocode into an html file, with proper formatting.

Problems

In the assignement we didn't really have any problems since we were just making comments into code. We would say the one thing we were struggling with was coming up with ideas for our games and seeing what should come after each steps.

Reflection

The assignment wasn't too difficult, as it didn't involve any real coding. Walter added music into the pseudocode, making it slightly more complex, but not at all difficult. Overall, was quite fun!
